For Current Students

To support the new Data Science minor and APS certificate, I will be teaching two core courses from their curriculum next semester. The courses I will be teaching are MAT 213: Elementary Statistics — providing foundational statistical skills — and CIS 360: Data Visualization, a new offering focused on data analysis and visual communication using tools such as Excel, Tableau, and Power BI. With both of these courses, I aim to equip you with both the conceptual understanding and practical, hands-on experience required to successfully use Data Science in real life.

Additionally, I will be teaching the MBA course BUS 630: Information Technology for Decision-Makers in Spring semester term B.

More information for both courses to come soon.
