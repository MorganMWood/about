Research

As a PhD candidate at the University of North Carolina Chapel Hill, my research is in the field of Operations Management. My interests lie in service operations and stochastic modeling & analysis. I am particularly interested in data-driven modeling and the management of service systems which I have explored through a collaboration with an international airport.
